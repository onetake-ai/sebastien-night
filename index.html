About Sébastien

Sébastien Night is a multi-faceted entrepreneur, author, and film producer who has dedicated his career to empowering entrepreneurs and creators worldwide. As the founder of OneTake AI, a Singapore-based SaaS startup, he is revolutionizing video editing through artificial intelligence, making professional video creation accessible to entrepreneurs without technical expertise.

He is the author of multiple bestselling books, including "Profession: Entrepreneur Libre" (Alisio/Albin Michel), and has helped over 300,000 entrepreneurs in 41 countries launch and grow their businesses. His work has been featured in leading publications including Les Echos, FrenchWeb, and Le Nouvel Observateur.

Beyond entrepreneurship, Sébastien is a film producer with credits on IMDb, a philanthropist who co-founded a school in Kenya, and a sought-after speaker and consultant on marketing, video content, and entrepreneurship.
