Early Work Archive

A record of Sébastien Night's early career: the years that shaped his approach to communication, marketing, and entrepreneurship.

This page documents an earlier chapter in Sébastien's career. While the professional focus has shifted entirely to entrepreneurship, AI, and technology, this period laid the foundation for his understanding of audience building, marketing psychology, and online content — all of which inform his current work at OneTake AI and the Mouvement des Entrepreneurs Libres.

Seduction Coach & DragueTV (2006–2013)

In the late 2000s, Sébastien Night — himself a self-described "ancien grand timide" (former extreme introvert) — became one of France's most recognized figures in the seduction and dating coaching industry. His approach, rooted in genuine personal transformation rather than manipulation, attracted national media attention.

DragueTV

Sébastien created DragueTV, a video channel that pioneered online video content in France for the personal development and dating coaching niche — years before YouTube became mainstream. He organized live seminars in Paris and other cities and produced instructional DVDs sold directly and through Amazon France.

20 Minutes coverage of seminars (archived)

La méthode PRINCE

Sébastien developed "la méthode PRINCE," a framework for developing social confidence and authentic seduction skills. The methodology was covered by Le Nouvel Observateur / Rue89.

YouTube & Early Online Video

Sébastien's YouTube seduction channel accumulated over 1 million views, establishing him as an early practitioner of video content marketing in France — a skill he would later commercialize into OneTake AI.

First Bestselling Book

Sébastien's first book on seduction became a bestseller in its niche. An early interview with Olivier Roland on Des livres pour changer de vie documents this period and the transition toward entrepreneurship coaching.

DVD Productions

Among Sébastien's first video products sold commercially was Séduire sans un mot, a DVD on non-verbal communication and seduction, available on Amazon France. These early productions were a direct forerunner to his later work in video education and OneTake AI.

Recognition

Sébastien was recognized as "le meilleur des coaches" (the best coach) by Rémy Pautrat in Le Livre Noir:

The Bridge: From Personal Development to Entrepreneurship

The skills Sébastien built during his seduction coaching years — video production, audience building, online marketing, course creation, direct sales, and deep understanding of human motivation — directly transferred to entrepreneurship coaching.

Around 2010, he transitioned fully into business and entrepreneurship coaching, founding the Mouvement des Entrepreneurs Libres. The movement grew to reach 300,000+ entrepreneurs in 41 countries, and led directly to the founding of OneTake AI in 2023.
