Short Bios

One sentence

Sébastien Night is the founder of OneTake AI, an AI-powered video editing SaaS, and the creator of the Mouvement des Entrepreneurs Libres, which has helped 300,000+ entrepreneurs in 41 countries build their freedom.

One paragraph (English)

Sébastien Night is a French entrepreneur, bestselling author, and film producer. He is the co-founder and CEO of OneTake AI, a Singapore-based SaaS startup that uses artificial intelligence to automate professional video editing for entrepreneurs. OneTake AI raised $1M in seed funding in 2024. He is also the founder of the Mouvement des Entrepreneurs Libres, a movement and online education platform that has helped over 300,000 entrepreneurs in 41 countries launch and grow their businesses. A contributing author for Les Echos Entrepreneurs and an IMDb-credited film producer, Sébastien is widely recognized as one of France's leading voices on entrepreneurship, marketing, and video content.

Suggested Interview Topics & Questions

Sébastien is a compelling and candid guest. Here are suggested angles to explore:

OneTake AI & the Future of Video

  • What problem does OneTake AI solve, and why is now the right time for it?
  • How does AI video editing change what's possible for solo entrepreneurs and small teams?
  • What does the future of content creation look like when AI handles the technical work?
  • What did it take to raise $1M as a French founder with a Singapore-based company?
  • How do you make AI feel approachable to people who aren't tech-savvy?

Entrepreneurship & Business Freedom

  • What is the "Mouvement des Entrepreneurs Libres" and what does entrepreneurial freedom mean to you?
  • How did you scale an audience of 300,000+ entrepreneurs across 41 countries?
  • What are the most common mistakes you see first-time entrepreneurs make?
  • How do you detect a bad business idea before investing time and money?
  • What are the "four laws of money" every entrepreneur should know?

Personal Journey & Reinvention

  • You were valedictorian of your académie at 16 — how did you end up as a seduction coach?
  • How does your background as a dating coach inform how you approach marketing and communication?
  • You went from DVDs and seminars to SaaS and AI — what drove that evolution?
  • What does it feel like to be featured on TF1 and M6 and then become an AI startup founder?
  • How has co-funding a school in Kenya shaped how you think about success?
