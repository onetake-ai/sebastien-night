Beyond Business Podcast
Podcast

"Revolutionizing Content Creation with OneTake AI"

Katherine Martín-Fisher introduces Sébastien Night and his company, One Take AI. They discuss overcoming clients' fear of AI and being on camera, as well as the development and marketing challenges faced by One Take AI. The conversation delves into evolving perceptions and the benefits of AI in content creation, comparing One Take AI with other video tools. They highlight the user experience and onboarding process, providing insights on implementing One Take AI in businesses. A client success story showcases the platform's video translation features.
