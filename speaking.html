Speaking Topics

AI and the Future of Video Content

How AI is transforming video production, making it accessible to every entrepreneur, and what this means for the future of content marketing. Insights from building OneTake AI.

Entrepreneurial Freedom — Building a Business Around Your Life

How to build a business that serves your life — not the other way around. Drawing on 15+ years building the Mouvement des Entrepreneurs Libres and helping 300,000+ entrepreneurs across 41 countries.

How solo entrepreneurs and small businesses can use video to grow an audience, generate leads, and sell their products — without a big team or technical skills.
