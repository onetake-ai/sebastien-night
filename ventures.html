OneTake AI

Founded August 2023 · Singapore · SaaS · B2B

Visit onetake.ai

OneTake Pte Ltd is a Singapore-based technology startup co-founded by Sébastien Night in August 2023. The company markets OneTake AI, a B2B SaaS platform that automates professional video editing through artificial intelligence.

OneTake AI serves SMEs worldwide — particularly entrepreneurs, authors, coaches, and content creators who want to produce and share professional-quality video content without technical skills or expensive equipment.

What OneTake AI Does

OneTake AI handles the entire video editing workflow automatically, in a single click. The platform includes:

  • Removes background noise for clean, professional audio
  • Cuts awkward silences and filler words automatically
  • Adds on-screen titles and highlights key points
  • Creates visual transitions at topic changes
  • Composes a unique, AI-generated background music track adapted to each video
  • Translates videos into the viewer's language for international reach
  • No technical skills required — designed for non-technical users

The platform also includes OneTake Chat, an AI writing assistant that helps users create course outlines, video scripts, and advertising copy.

Target Customers

OneTake AI is built for:

  • Entrepreneurs, authors, and trainers (online and in-person)
  • Content creators who want to grow an audience through video
  • Solo business owners and small teams
  • Coaches, consultants, and educators selling training products
  • Anyone who wants professional video quality without being technical
